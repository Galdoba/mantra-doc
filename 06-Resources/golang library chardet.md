### **Описание:** 
chardet is library to automatically detect [charset](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Character_encoding) of texts for [Go programming language](http://golang.org/). It's based on the algorithm and data in [ICU](http://icu-project.org/)'s implementation.
See [pkgdoc](https://pkg.go.dev/github.com/saintfish/chardet)
